About

Abikriti is a full service, Indo-centric, Event Planning & Designing company based in Bangalore, India. Abikriti is an offshoot of Blazon, a 15 year old “veteran” company experienced in 300-plus successful product launches of global giants like Nike, ASICS, Tanishq etc.


Founder of Abikriti & Owner of Blazon
Anupama Hariprakash is a multilingual, creative-entrepreneur, passionate about understanding her clients’ needs thereon from concept to completion to create a unique unforgettable event. This nuanced creative perspective combined with her successful management of Blazon’s team of experts makes her uniquely positioned to bring forth her client’s aesthetic vision to life.


Anupama, initially began as freelancer to help design, create and essentially bring-to-life, the Indian themed cultural events of her artist friends living abroad. Their enthusiastic response and growth of new customers by personal recommendation encouraged the birth of ABIKRITI (a blend of her daughters’ names)


We, at Abikriti provide our clients with spectacular design-specific events through creativity and uniqueness based on their individual tastes and preferences. We incorporate themes, design layouts, custom make props for decor, print, package exclusive invites, brochures etc., curated stage backdrops, outsource from other vendors for one-of-a-kind return gifts. Our ultimate goal is transform your space with your unique story.
