About

Abikriti is an Indo-centric design space based in Bangalore, India. Which solely focuses on delivering visual communication solutions with an artistic and cultural perspective. Abikriti is an offshoot of Blazon, a well-established, 15 plus-year-old visual branding solutions firm with many renowned clients such as Nike, Adidas, Asics, Tanishq, etc.


Founder of Abikriti, Anupama Hariprakash is a multilingual creative entrepreneur with expertise in all aspects of successful cultural event, program planning, designing, development, and management.


Anupama initially began as a freelancer to accommodate multiple personal requests of her friends to design and provide a one stop solution for Indian based weddings/cultural/dance events for artists living overseas. The enthusiastic reception of her revolutionary designs and growth through word-of-mouth referrals led to the birth of Abikriti, in 2014, named after her daughters. This nuanced creative perspective, combined with her successful experiences in managing a team of skilled graphic designers and installation experts at Blazon, makes Anupama uniquely positioned to bring your aesthetic vision to life. Abikriti specializes in concept, design, print, package, and deliver custom invites, brochures, curated stage backdrops, exclusive props, presentation, source dance costumes, accessories like jewelry, one of a kind return gifts/ favors.
